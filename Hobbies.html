Gardening

When shelter-in-place set in, I was faced with much more time on my hands, so I began to pick up a new hobby: gardening. I love making my backyard prettier, and this one of the many projects I've undertaken. I painted these barrels and planted flowers in them. I would highly recommend anyone to garden, even if they feel that they don't have a green thumb.
